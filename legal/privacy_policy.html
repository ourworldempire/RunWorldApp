Privacy Policy

App: RunWorld  |  Developer: Ritanjay  |  Last updated: 8 May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use RunWorld, we collect the following information:

  • Account data: Name, email address, and avatar emoji you provide during sign-up.
  • Google Sign-In (optional): If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates during an active run session to calculate distance, trace your route, and determine which city zones you have run through. Location is collected only while the app is in use and a run is active.
  • Fitness data: Step counts (via device pedometer), distance, duration, calories, and pace for each run session you complete.
  • Device data: Push notification token to send you run reminders and social activity alerts (optional, requires permission).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To record your run sessions and display your fitness progress.
  • To power the territory map feature — attributing captured zones to your account.
  • To populate leaderboards, friend feeds, and challenge progress.
  • To send push notifications you have opted into.
  • To calculate XP, level, and achievements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as follows:

  • Supabase (database): Your data is stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database (EU region). Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.
  • Google Sign-In: If used, Google receives your sign-in request per Google's own privacy policy.
  • Other users: Your name, avatar, level, XP, and territory percentage are visible on public leaderboards and friend profiles. You can set your profile to private in Settings → Privacy.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

5. Children's Privacy

RunWorld is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Permissions

  • Location (always/while in use): Required for GPS route tracking during runs.
  • Physical activity / Motion: Required to count steps via the device pedometer.
  • Internet: Required to sync run data, load maps, and connect with friends.
  • Notifications (optional): For run reminders and social activity updates.

7. Security

All API communication uses HTTPS. Authentication tokens are stored in Android's encrypted secure storage (EncryptedSharedPreferences). We use short-lived access tokens (15 minutes) with rotating refresh tokens (30 days).

8. Your Rights

You may at any time: access your data (visible in the app), correct your name or avatar (Settings → Edit Profile), or delete your account and all data (Settings → Delete Account). For other requests, email us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified via in-app message. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
